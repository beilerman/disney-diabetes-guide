Tips for a Healthy Park Experience

Prioritize Protein

Especially if you're on a GLP-1 medication, focus on protein-rich dishes to increase satiety. Look for the ðŸ§ª marker on our guide for suggestions like grilled meats, fish, and plant-based proteins.

Plan Your Portions

Portion sizes can be large. Sharing entrees or choosing snacks can help manage carb intake. Use the Nutrition Tracker to monitor your intake and plan your insulin doses accordingly.

Be Mindful of Alcohol

If you choose to drink, be aware that alcohol can cause delayed hypoglycemia. Never drink on an empty stomach and monitor your blood sugar closely, even hours later.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water. Dehydration can affect blood sugar levels, and staying hydrated can also help with feelings of fullness. Carry a water bottle and refill it often.
